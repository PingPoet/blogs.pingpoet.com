New Jacket.

Someone asked me about getting a new Jacket. I guess I am not really an expert at outdoor clothing, but, I have always had a strong interest and have activly used outdoor clothing for many years. Duh, like no one else ever used outdoor clothing.

I bought a pair of Merrell boots over a year ago. They use Gore-Tex and are therefore water proof with no treating. Oh, and the stuff lets moisture out, which is very important for shoes.
Great Stuff. BTW: It is Merrell Technology is their name. Not Merrell shoes or boots or outdoor clothing, and their site looks cool.

 Gore-Tex® XCR™ is the latest cool stuff. It is a thrid generation of GoreTex.
I strongly reccomend getting GoreTex of some sort. There are 2 & 3 layer Gore-Tex fabrics that are incrementally better.


The Best(?) Jackets are  made with Gore-Tex® XCR™
I think the best choice is the 2-layer Gore-Tex as it is more breathable than thre 3-layer.
A well made Jacket with Gore-Tex® XCR™ and a zip-in fleece liner could be used at least three seasons of the year.
