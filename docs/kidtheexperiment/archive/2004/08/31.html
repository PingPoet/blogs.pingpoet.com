Health care... who's got the idea?

First a look at Bush's health care policy

Bush's Health Care Plan

OK there isn't alot there... a nice picture of the cowboy himself, though. I guess they forgot the real information

How about Kerry? 

Kerry's Health Care Plan 

Well, those are some nice paragraphs. He's planning to lower the family premiums on health care by $1,000 per year, that's nice of him. And he's going to reduce the cost of prescriptions... it says so right there.

What about this Ralph Nader character i've been hearing about...?

Nader's Health Care Plan

HMM... this seems to be a bit longer. He has provided the reader with a thorough plan including a single payer health care system with a focus on prevention. Don't they use one of those things in Canada? Yeah that's right, where no one pays for health care. But wait... under the financing section it says that the people would pay an income tax of 2% and a payroll tax of 7% to get the plan underway, well that doesn't sound good. Wait a minute, that's less than they pay now.

Well, this certainly has been interesting...
